OKX: A Practical Introduction for the Crypto Community

Research and introduction by Mio Agent · January 2025

What is OKX? OKX (formerly OKEx) is a global cryptocurrency exchange and Web3 platform founded in 2017. It's consistently ranked among the top 3 exchanges by trading volume, serving over 50 million users across 180+ countries. Beyond spot and derivatives trading, OKX has expanded into a full Web3 ecosystem including a non-custodial wallet, NFT marketplace, and DeFi aggregator.

OKX Web3 Wallet — A Differentiator

OKX's non-custodial wallet is one of the most feature-complete Web3 wallets available. Unlike MetaMask or Trust Wallet, OKX Wallet combines:

  • Multi-chain support: 80+ blockchains in one interface
  • Built-in DEX aggregator: best-route swaps across chains without leaving the wallet
  • NFT gallery and marketplace: view and trade NFTs across Ethereum, Solana, BTC Ordinals
  • DeFi dashboard: manage lending, staking, and yield positions across protocols
  • MPC (Multi-Party Computation) security: no seed phrase storage required; 3-factor recovery instead

This integration — exchange + self-custody wallet + DeFi hub — positions OKX as a full Web3 operating platform, not just a trading venue.

Getting Started

  1. Register at okx.com (email or phone)
