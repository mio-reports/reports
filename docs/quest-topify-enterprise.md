## FAQs

### 1. What does Topify actually track?
Topify tracks how your brand appears in AI-generated answers across engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Bing Copilot, and Google AI Overview, with coverage varying by plan.

### 2. How is GEO different from SEO?
SEO helps pages rank in classic search results. GEO helps your brand get cited, compared, and recommended inside AI-generated answers during discovery and evaluation.

### 3. Why does Enterprise matter if I already have dashboards?
Enterprise adds custom integrations, API access, dedicated support, and the workflow depth needed to turn visibility data into repeatable operating motions across large teams.

### 4. Can agencies and in-house teams use the same workspace?
Yes. Topify positions itself for agencies, SaaS teams, and startups, and its pricing emphasizes unlimited team seats so collaboration is not the bottleneck.

### 5. How quickly can results move?
Topify's public case studies cite early gains such as +40% visibility in 3 days and +35% uplift in 2 days, but timing depends on category, prompt set, and execution quality.

### 6. What makes Topify different from manual AI prompt checking?
Manual checks are slow, inconsistent, and impossible to scale across brands. Topify turns prompt tracking, competitor benchmarking, and optimization into a repeatable system.
